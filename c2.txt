Pin the KO6 evolution gates as reachable against the fixed counter

The Mega-only counter (v0.2.466) is 2.6x stricter than what shipped, and 100
evolution rules hang off it -- 88 into Mega, 12 into Ultimate. Audited all of
them; no code change needed, but the reachability now rests on facts that were
never written down, so they get pinned.

Combat is an HP RACE: BASE_ATTACK is a flat 5 for Rookie..Mega, so both sides
deal ~5/round and whoever has more HP wins. The pet's HP cap is AGE-gated
(HEALTH_CAP_LADDER tops out at 30 at 13+ game-days) while an early random Mega
has 25. So an aged, HP-trained Ultimate out-lasts a Mega (58% at 25 HP/60 power,
71-100% at 30 HP) and a young one literally cannot touch it (0% at 15 HP). That
IS the gate: it demands investment rather than luck. Lifespan is not a squeeze --
LIFE_START is ~3 real days against ~5.2 real hours to reach the HP cap.

No coverage holes. "KO6 0" means EXACTLY zero, so a node pairing a `KO6 0` exit
with a `KO6 2+` exit would strand a pet on exactly one kill -- it would match
neither branch and never evolve. Zero nodes have that shape. The 66 single-exit
Mega nodes strand only at 0-1 kills, which is the gate working, not a hole.

The two tightest gates make a CHAMPION fell a Mega (verX Greymon X ->
MetalGreymon X, x3a -> Cyberdramon X). Reachable: their only exit is that rule,
so the pet simply stays Champion, ages into the HP ladder, and retries -- a lost
bout costs lifespan, not the run.
